Only one day in Rio? It can be done — and done well. "A Day in Rio" is the
itinerary I built to discover — or drink, as they say here — the juice of the
Cidade Maravilhosa in eight hours, with a private car or van with driver and
pick-up straight from your hotel.

## The itinerary

1. **Christ the Redeemer** — up on the Corcovado rack railway, 20 minutes
   through the forest, into the embrace of one of the 7 wonders of the modern
   world and a 360° view over the city.
2. **Santa Teresa** — the bohemian quarter: historic houses, workshops,
   graffiti and the Largo dos Guimarães.
3. **The Selarón Steps** — the story of the extravagant artist who covered
   them in tiles from all over the world.
4. **Sugarloaf** — the cable car from Praia Vermelha via Morro da Urca:
   facing Christ, Guanabara Bay and Copacabana from an angle worthy of a
   painting.
